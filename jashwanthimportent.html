Few years before, my uncle was a border soldier in Kashmir near U.R.I. base camp but one day we came to know that he was missing, during Kashmir insurgency. According to official figures released in Jammu and Kashmir assembly, there were 4,300 disappearance cases and more than 47,000 people died due to lack of medical facilities in time. One of the reasons behind all these is the communication gap between the soldiers during the mission and the military base and Military base camp are also unaware of the soldier's physical condition during the mission.So, I have developed an idea to get rid of these problems. A jacket will be provided to every army officer,(the jacket will be like a balloon) the jacket will contain an inert gas inside it with an M.Q. sensor (which will help to sense the presence of the inert gas). When a soldier will get injured with a bullet or with knife than the gas in the jacket will release and hence, the M.Q. sensor will not able to sense the gas, and therefore it will immediately send a message (using nodeMCU as an IoT device) to Army base camp to take the required action.Any person from the army base camp can see the location of each soldier at any time as a G.P.S. Module is also fitted in the soldier’s jacket. Whenever a soldier will go far a particular distance from the war zone it will automatically send a message (using nodeMCU) with his exact location to the army base to come for help. We can see the soldier’s real-time heart rate (using a heart rate sensor) and his body temperature understand the soldier’s physical condition.

          With the help of this device the person from army base camp can directly  

          communicate with a single soldier/many soldiers at the time of emergency.
